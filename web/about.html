Our Story

Two ideal minds with thoughtful innovation

Rajeev and Puhup, two friends who work at Deloitte, decided to venture into entrepreneurship by starting Tea-Pot Tales. Despite of full-time jobs in the tech industry, our shared passion for business and desire to try something new led us to pursue this idea. Our story highlights the balance between a stable job and entrepreneurship, showcasing how passion and creativity can lead to new ventures, even for those already working in a different field.

Our Vision

To create a welcoming community space that blends tradition with innovation, where every cup of tea inspires connection and refreshment. We aim to provide the finest quality tea, crafted with care, while fostering a culture of warmth, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Through our passion for tea and entrepreneurship, we seek to grow our business into a beloved local destination, offering a unique experience that bridges our expertise in IT with our love for creating memorable moments.
